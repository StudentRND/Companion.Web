CodeDay Chat
For communication during CodeDay, we've implemented a chat system so that staff, volunteers, and attendees can all talk to each other both during, before, and after CodeDay. You should have received an email with instructions on how to join it, but all you have to do is go to chat.srnd.org and sign in with your s5 account. With the CodeDay Chat, you will be able to receive mentorship, help, contact staff, and talk with CodeDay attendees from all over North America!
If you don't have an s5 account, you can sign up for one by going to s5.srnd.org. Your s5 account is your login for all things StudentRND, so dont forget to make one!
