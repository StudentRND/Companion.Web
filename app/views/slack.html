CodeDay Chat
At CodeDay, we use Slack for communication during CodeDay. You should have received an email with instructions on how to join it. With the CodeDay Slack, you will be able to receive mentorship, contact staff, and chat with attendees from CodeDays all over the nation!
To sign up for our Slack team, you need to make an s5 account, then tap on the "CodeDay Chat" link once you're signed in.
